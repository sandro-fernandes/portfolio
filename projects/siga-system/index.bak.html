SIGA System.

Details.

SIGA is the name of a project that encomprises several digital touch points of a complete and new queue system to be used by the entire administrative services of a portuguese University.

The tickets themselves, the digital touchpoints where they are taken, either in person on a tablet or via the University's own mobile app, the TV interfaces and the backoffice to manage the entire service were designed from scratch.

Roles: User Experience, User Interface Design & Front-end development
